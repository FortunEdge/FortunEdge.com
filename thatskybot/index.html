ThatSkyBot

ThatSkyBot is a Twitter bot that retweets and promotes content related to Sky: Children of the Light.

Twitter Page

Features

  • Retweets tweets with the hashtags related to Sky: Children of the Light.
    Currently tracking: #thatskygame and #sky星を紡ぐ子どもたち
  • Filters high-volume and low-interaction content like spam and links to other websites, based on filter lists and rate-limiting retweets by user.
